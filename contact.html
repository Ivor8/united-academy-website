Frequently Asked Questions

How do I apply?

You can apply by visiting our campus in Simbock with the required documents: CNI photocopy, diploma, 2 photos, and registration fee. You can also start the process by contacting us by phone or email.

What are the tuition fees?

Please contact us directly for detailed fee information. We offer competitive pricing and flexible installment plans. Registration fee is 25,000 FCFA for all programs.

Do you offer scholarships?

We have limited scholarships for outstanding students from disadvantaged backgrounds. Contact our office for more information.

Are internships guaranteed?

Yes, every student completes a 3-5 month internship in one of our partner clinics, pharmacies, or agencies. We help place you.

What diplomas do you offer?

We prepare students for DQP (Diplôme de Qualification Professionnelle) and CQP (Certificat de Qualification Professionnelle), recognized nationally by MINEFOP under agreement N° 00300/MINEFOP/SG/DFOP/SDGSF/CSACD/CBAC.

Can I visit the campus?

Absolutely! We welcome visitors during office hours. You can tour our labs, classrooms, and meet our team.
